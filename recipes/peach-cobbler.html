Peach Cobbler

Description

Classic peach cobbler is always a hit! Juicy peaches topped with biscuit-like dough, sprinkled cinnamon sugar...
Is your mouth watering yet? Lets get started!


Ingredients

  • 8 fresh peaches - peeled, pitted and sliced into thin wedges
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small pieces
  • ¼ cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Mix together 3 tablespoons white sugar and 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon. Leave this for the end!
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  3. n a large bowl, combine peaches, ¼ cup white sugar, ¼ cup brown sugar, ¼ teaspoon cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, and cornstarch. Toss to coat evenly, and pour into a 2 quart baking dish. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine flour, ¼ cup white sugar, ¼ cup brown sugar, baking powder, and salt. Blend in butter with your fingertip, or a pastry blender, until mixture resembles coarse meal. Stir in the water until just combined.
  5. Remove peaches from oven, and drop spoonfuls of topping over them. Sprinkle entire cobbler with the sugar and cinnamon mixture from step one. Bake until topping is golden, about thirty minutes.
  6. Enjoy
